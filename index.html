Miloš Milivojević

I am a senior enterprise Java developer with a Master's degree in Software Engineering and 4 years of experience in developing software - predominantly e-Commerce and financial sector - based on JVM technology stack and a main focus on the backend.

The applications that I've built focus on making sure requirements get results. I strive to design and develop technology solutions that solve problems and create opportunities. As a software enthusiast and overall geek, I tend to listen, learn and always deliver.

The types of projects that I've worked on vary from large systems like internet banking, pre-bankruptcy settlements or an international money-transfer system to small ones, like a web-service based template engine, effective interest rate calculator or an idea management software.
